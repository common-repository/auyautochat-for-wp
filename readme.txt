Autochat - Automatic chat conversation 24x7!
The easiest way to stay in touch with your customers and get more leads!

== Description ==

Its a Mobile Adaptative WordPress Plugin to encourage your customers to write you through your Website 24 x 7.
It works like a contact form but the questions are asked one by one. There are about 6 questions among which are the name of the client, the query to be made and contact information such as email and telephone.

More and better leads.
Increases leads with existing traffic, since customers are encouraged to ask questions directly chatting.
The button also automatically, opens a new conversation after a few seconds to the visitor, so that he or her is encouraged to check the message and respond.

Mobile adaptative.
The Autochat Automatic Conversation for websites developed by Autochat.uy allows you to embed it natively on your site, so that the user naturally makes questions.

Friendly installation.
You do not need to know how to program. By installing the plugin on your Wordpress site and configuring the message and your phone, you will start receiving leads about your products and services.

Available in 12 languages. 
By default, the language in which the plugin options are displayed will be the language of your wordpress site panel if available or English.

Full conversation:

- "Hi. What can I do for you?"
- "How may I help you?"
- "It will be a pleasure to assist you, what is your name?"
- "I am in charge of contacting an advisor to answer your question"
- "What is your email?"
- "Could I also ask your phone number? Please include the area code."
- "When do you prefer to be called?"
- "Perfect!. With the information that you have given me, an advisor will contact you shortly. Do you want to leave me any additional questions?"
- "Excellent! I will contact the advisor and give him all this information."
- "Thank you very much for contacting us!"

== Installation ==

Follow this steps:
 - Download the plugin.
 - Open WordPress admin, go to Plugins, click Add New. Then upload Plugin
 - Click select file button and upload the .zip file.
 - Activate & open plugin’s settings page located in the main WP admin menu as “Auy Autochat”
 
Or if needed, upload manually:
 - Download the plugin.
 - Unzip it and upload to /wp-content/plugins/
 - Open WordPress admin – Plugins and click “Activate” next to the plugin
 - Activate & open plugin’s settings page located in the main WP admin menu as “Auy Autochat”

The plugin suports some translations. By default labels and texts 
Check out our [documentation](https://autochat.uy/autochat-setup/) to make the most of our Autochat plugin.
